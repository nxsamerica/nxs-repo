Nexas Webinars

Don't miss our free Webinars!

Nexas America's webinars are a convenient way to learn more about our products and how they can benefit your shop. We generally have 2 different types of webinars:

7-Minute Webinar Series™

Our 7-Minute Webinars provide a short presentation that lasts approximately 7 minutes. You get to learn a lot, in a short period of time. We repeat the presentation 4 times in a half hour and you can join in or leave at any time.

Full Product Webinars

Our Full Product Webinars generally last between 30-90 minutes. They give you more time to learn about the benefits of our product and to ask our technical specialists any questions you may have.
