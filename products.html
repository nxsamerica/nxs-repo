Hardware

Nexas hardware keeps you connected to all of your machines whether you need Serial, Ethernet or Wireless capabilities. We provide quality products with competitive pricing from third party vendors such as Moxa and Comtrol as well as our own in-house hardware such as the ORiON™ CNC Productivity Hub.



MTConnect®

MTConnect® is the communication standard of choice for manufacturing. This is why our products are 100% MTConnect® compliant. The MTConnect® standard enables manufacturing equipment to provide data in structured XML rather than proprietary formats.

Fusion™

Fusion™ Production Document Organizer is a cutting edge Production Document Management (PDM) System with DNC capabilities that gives you quick, easy and intuitive control over all your Production and DNC Data.

DataXchange™

DataXchange™ is a Cloud and On-Premise machine monitoring system that increases overall equipment effectiveness and shop floor productivity. Compatible with a wide variety of machines including CNC’s, PLC’s, welders, and manual machines.
